#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Tassili_n'Ajjer,190048|WD~Tassili_n'Ajjer,190048]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Tassili n'Ajjer (Berber: Tassili n Ajjer, Arabic: طاسيلي ناجر, romanized: ṭāssīlī naʾjir; "Plateau of rivers") 
> is a mountain range in the Sahara desert, located in south-eastern Algeria. 
> 
> It holds one of the most important groupings of prehistoric cave art in the world 
> and covers an area of more than 72,000 km2 (28,000 sq mi).
>
> The site has been designated a national park and a Biosphere Reserve, 
> and was inducted into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list in 1982.
>
> The literal English translation of Tassili n'Ajjer is 'plateau of rivers'.
>
